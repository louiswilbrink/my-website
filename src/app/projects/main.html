FASTER Scale

www.fasterscale.io

Tech Stack: AngularJS, Firebase, Angular-Material, Node, Grunt.

The FASTER Scale app is an automated, cloud-based tool based on the addiction recovery worksheet created by Michael Dye.

The worksheet helps to identify anxiety before a relapse. It's a remarkable system that helps people on their journey through recovery.

Contract Timer

contract-timer.louiswilbrink.com

Tech Stack: AngularJS, Firebase, Angular-Material, Node, Gulp.

This is a quick app I made to demonstrate some of the components included in the Angular-Material library. I particularly like the toast feature, the fab buttons, and the Side Navigation component. Angular Material is more than a simple library. It includes services as well as a layout and grid system that I have implemented in this app as well.
